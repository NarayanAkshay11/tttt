About Us

Archimedes Autonomous Systems is a cutting-edge research initiative at Nanyang Technological University, focusing on developing advanced maritime autonomous systems.

Our mission is to revolutionize the maritime industry through innovative technologies and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our Goals

  • Develop state-of-the-art autonomous navigation systems
  • Enhance maritime safety and efficiency
  • Reduce environmental impact of maritime operations
  • Foster collaboration between academia and industry
